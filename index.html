Saudi-Dialect-ALLaM

Code-only release for LoRA fine-tuning of ALLaM-7B for Saudi dialect generation (Hijazi & Najdi). Includes training scripts, evaluation (dialect classifier, chrF++, BERTScore, diversity), inference helpers, and analysis notebooks. Dialect-token variant: 84.21% Saudi alignment, 6.21% MSA leakage (base 47.97% / 32.63%). No dataset or weights released—reproducible on your own data.

PyTorch LoRA ALLaM-7B Reproducible Research

Aug 2025 · Paper (arXiv) · First-author, RCUSM Jeddah

Ketaba-OCR at AR-MS NakbaNLP 2026

Parameter-efficient Arabic HTR for historical manuscripts (Omar Al-Saleh Memoir Collection, Ruq'ah/Naskh). QLoRA fine-tuning of Sherif’s Arabic–English HTR model with DoRA and RSLoRA; Linear+Boost weighted ensemble of six variants. 1st on per-line evaluation (CER 0.0819, WER 0.2588) and 3rd on official corpus-wide (CER 0.0938, WER 0.2996). Co-authored with NAMAA Community.

Python QLoRA DoRA HTR
